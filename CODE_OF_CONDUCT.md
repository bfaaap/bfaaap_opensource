# Code of Conduct — the bFaaaP community

> 🌐 **English** · [日本語](i18n/ja/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) · [Deutsch](i18n/de/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)

**Welcome to the meeting place of technology and music.**

bFaaaP is an open, inclusive project: a foot‑free piano pedal so that anyone can play. Our
community — in **[GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/bfaaap/bfaaap_opensource/discussions)** —
is a place where everyone who loves technology **and** music can exchange knowledge, share their
creativity, and lift each other up, in safety. This is **assistive technology**; empathy is our
foundation. Please help us grow a good community by following these guidelines.

## Our promises

1. **Be respectful.** We welcome people of every background, ability, and level of experience. No
   personal attacks, harassment, discrimination, or belittling of beginners.
2. **Be constructive.** Answer kindly; critique the idea, not the person. Aim for dialogue where
   everyone can learn and grow. A basic question is always welcome — we were all beginners once.
3. **Keep it safe.** Do not share content that infringes copyright or privacy, and do not post
   inappropriate content. If you see a troubling post, please use the **Report** button.
4. **Keep it relevant.** Post in the right category — **🔧 Build & Tech**, **🎹 Music**, or
   **💬 General** — and leave official notices to **📣 Announcements**. Sharing your own lessons,
   tuning service, or concerts is welcome in **General** or your self‑introduction, in moderation —
   but please don't turn an answer into a sales pitch.

## Not welcome here

Spam and advertising, phishing/malware links, off‑topic posts, hostile or uncivil behaviour,
harassment, doxxing, and **automated or bulk posting** (bots or AI scrapers mass‑posting) are hidden
or removed and will not be answered.

## For our music categories

- **Beginners are welcome.** Ask anything, however basic. Veterans: please be generous and kind.
- **Copyright & scores.** Do **not** upload copyrighted scores, lyrics, or audio (in whole or part)
  without the rightsholder's permission. Prefer legal/public‑domain resources (e.g. IMSLP).
- **Performance videos.** If other people appear in your video, get their permission first. For
  covers, please mind the song's copyright.
- **Promotion.** Sharing your lessons, tuning service, or concerts is welcome — in **💬 General** or
  your self‑introduction, in moderation. Please don't turn an answer into a sales pitch.

## Any language is welcome

Post in **Japanese, English, or German** — whatever is easiest for you. Ponte and Harmonia add a
collapsible reference translation into the other two languages under your post, so everyone can
follow along. **The original is authoritative; the AI translation is only a courtesy** — if anything
reads wrong, tell us (`@Ponte @Harmonia`) and we'll fix it.

## How this is moderated (human‑first)

Real people run this community, and **people make the final call** — that's what keeps answers
trustworthy on things that matter (wiring, 24 V power, radio rules, assistive‑device safety).

- **Owners** (Shishido, Taguchi, Tanaka) are the final deciders: they appoint moderators, handle
  serious violations, and set policy.
- **Moderators** are trusted members who patrol day‑to‑day, tidy threads, and take minor actions.
- A light **monthly on‑duty rota** (not around‑the‑clock) keeps moderation reliable, while the AI
  assistants patrol every day so the load on people stays gentle.
- **Steps:** anyone can **Report** → the on‑duty moderator handles obvious spam/violations
  immediately (a comment may be **hidden**, not deleted, with a short reason; a heated or resolved
  thread may be **locked**) → judgement calls are discussed among moderators → serious cases
  (e.g. blocking a user) are escalated to the owners.
- **AI assistants** (Ponte and Harmonia) are part of the team: they welcome newcomers, help keep
  conversations going, translate posts, flag risky ones, summarise long threads, and **draft**
  replies for a human to review. They sign every post and add a note when information should be
  checked by an expert. On anything touching **safety, medicine, electricity, rights/law, or an
  unsettled project decision** they defer to a human — and **AI never deletes, hides, or blocks on
  its own. A human presses every final button.**

## Enforcement

Where behaviour seriously breaks these guidelines, moderators may hide or remove posts and, to keep
the community safe, temporarily or permanently suspend an account. We aim to be **welcoming but
firm**, and — if a post is merely unclear — to **ask, not reject**.

## Scope & contact

This applies to all bFaaaP community spaces (Discussions, issues, pull requests). To report a
concern privately, contact the owners at <shishid@saaipf.com>.

This is **version 1** of our Code of Conduct — it draws on established open‑source codes (the
[Berlin Code of Conduct](https://berlincodeofconduct.org/) and the Contributor Covenant), adapted
for our music‑and‑technology community, and will keep evolving together with our moderators.

**Let's build the best community — together.** 🌉🎹
