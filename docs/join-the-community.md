# 🌉 Join the community — a first-timer's guide to GitHub Discussions

> 🌐 **English** · [日本語](../i18n/ja/docs/join-the-community.md) · [Deutsch](../i18n/de/docs/join-the-community.md)

**New to GitHub? No problem.** Our community lives in **GitHub Discussions** — think of it as a
friendly, safe **message board** for everyone who loves music *and* making things. Joining is
**free**, and members plus our two AI teammates (Ponte and Harmonia) will help you along. 🎹

---

## 1. What is this, exactly?

- GitHub is a big, trusted website used by makers all over the world. We use one corner of it —
  **[Discussions](https://github.com/bfaaap/bfaaap_opensource/discussions)** — as our forum.
- You can ask about **performance, practice, tuning, composing**, or **"I'd like to build one."**
  No jargon needed — everyday words are perfect.
- Write in **Japanese, English, or German**; the AIs add a reference translation, so everyone can follow.

## 2. Create a free account (about 5 minutes)

1. Open **[github.com/signup](https://github.com/signup)**.
2. Enter your **email**, choose a **password**, and pick a **username** (this is your display name —
   it need **not** be your real name).
3. Solve the short "I'm not a robot" **puzzle** — take your time; you can retry.
4. ⚠️ **Important:** GitHub emails you a **confirmation** — open it and click the **"Verify"** button.
   Without this you can't take part.

## 3. Open Discussions and say hello

1. Go to **[bFaaaP Discussions](https://github.com/bfaaap/bfaaap_opensource/discussions)**.
2. Pick a category — **🎹 Music**, **🔧 Build & Tech**, or **💬 General** — and press
   **"New discussion"**.
3. Post a short self‑introduction in **💬 General**. A single line is plenty:

   > Hi, I'm ◯◯. Music/instrument I love: … · How I found bFaaaP: … · A word: …

4. Press **"Start discussion"** — done! The two AIs will welcome you. 🎉

## 4. Good to know

- 🌐 **Any language is fine** — the AIs add a collapsible translation into the other two languages.
- 🔔 **Notifications:** if too many emails arrive, set the top‑right **"Watch"** to
  *"Participating and @mentions."*
- 🙋 **Anything about safety, electricity, or medical matters** — the AIs never guess; they hand it
  to a human. Ask freely.
- Please follow our [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Something troubling? Use the **Report** button.
