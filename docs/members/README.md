# bFaaaP members

The people behind bFaaaP (the **Platanus** group). Portraits are the project's own
hand‑drawn caricatures from <https://bfaaap.com/メンバー紹介/>, illustrated by
**Saki Shiokawa (塩川紗季)**. *(Personal contact details from the source pages are
intentionally omitted here.)*

---

### 大瀧 雅寛 — Masahiro Ootaki — *design & coordination*

![Masahiro Ootaki](ootaki-masahiro.jpg)

A **first‑class architect** specializing in barrier‑free housing. He is bFaaaP's
**designer and overall coordinator** — a key person who brought the team together,
and the one whose wheelchair‑using client's request started the whole project.

---

### 宍戸 知行 — Tomoyuki Shishido — *iOS app*

![Tomoyuki Shishido](shishido-tomoyuki.jpg)

**Ph.D. (Molecular Oncology)** and a **patent attorney (弁理士)**; currently in the
**Ph.D. program** of the Department of Information and Communications Engineering,
School of Engineering, **Institute of Science Tokyo** (東京科学大学).
ORCID: <https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8944-2088>.
He loves the piano (a self‑described beginner who started at 45) and leads the
**iOS** side of bFaaaP, having learned **Swift** to build it.

---

### 成澤 博行 — Hiroyuki Narusawa — *pedal device / firmware*

![Hiroyuki Narusawa](narusawa-hiroyuki.jpg)

Runs **Naru Science Soft** (electronic‑device development & manufacturing); a
graduate of Shibaura Institute of Technology (electrical engineering). He
**designs and builds the pedal device and its firmware**.

---

### 山口 恭子 — Kyoko Yamaguchi — *piano & pedagogy*

![Kyoko Yamaguchi](yamaguchi-kyoko.jpg)

A **piano instructor** (~15 years, mainly beginners) who has also directed a
children's choir that performed in Vienna. She champions a new, IT‑era pedal
technique so more people can enjoy the expressive power of the pedal.

---

### 徳重 大輔 — Daisuke Tokushige — *intellectual property*

![Daisuke Tokushige](tokushige-daisuke.jpg)

A **patent engineer (特許技術者)** at SK Patent firm who supports bFaaaP on the
**intellectual‑property** side (the project's patents).

---

### kana — *piano tuner*

![kana](kana.png)

A **piano tuner**, raised by musician parents. She studied performance, then chose
tuning to see the piano "from a different angle." She reflects deeply on how the
pedal and finger technique together shape a phrase — and sees bFaaaP as the
embodiment of that ideal.

---

### Fehmiju Fati — *composer / computer music*

![Fehmiju Fati](member2020.png)

A composer from the former Yugoslavia: accordion & piano from age 8, violin and
composition at Istanbul (MA in musicology), many international awards. Came to
Japan in 2015, studied computer music at Kunitachi College of Music (doctoral
course), and joined **KORG**'s sound‑data development in 2019.

---

### Midori — *music & healthcare*

![Midori](midori.png)

Started piano at about 5. She now works in **healthcare**; a piano nearby calms
her. Moved by Shishido's period‑style (non‑sustained) Sarabande, she looks forward
to expressive sustained sound through bFaaaP — *"bFaaaP may switch on the heart, too."*

---

### 田中 遥斗 — Haruto Tanaka — *electrical engineering*

![Haruto Tanaka](tanaka.jpg)

At **Tokyo Tech (Electrical & Electronic)**, a guitarist in the Tokyo Tech
Platanus club. He provides **technical support** and wants to extend bFaaaP Switch
to instruments beyond the piano.

---

### 田口 — Taguchi — *software engineering*

![Taguchi](taguchi.jpg)

A **software engineer** deepening his understanding of bFaaaP with Narusawa's
guidance. He aims to build the skills to improve the product from user feedback —
and would love to learn the piano himself.
